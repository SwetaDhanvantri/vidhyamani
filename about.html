ABOUT US

Vidhyamani is a platform which is specially made for learners and instructors. Vidhyamani is about bringing people together. Our mission is to opening new possibilities to all the people, so that they can enhance their knowledge. Vidhyamani comes up with the lot of opportunities to both learners and instructors.

Meaning of “Vidhyamani”

The word “Vidhyamani” is the combination of two words “Vidhya” (knowledge) and “mani” (precious stone) i.e. treasure of knowledge.

Vidhyamani logo represents

In Vidhyamani logo a bird eagle defines determination & focus; V in logo defines “vidhya” which means “knowledge” & M defines “mani” which means “jewel” & together it makes “Vidhyamani”. Just like an eagle, we want learners occupy full wisdom and explore their sky of knowledge. Our aim is to give the sky of knowledge and prepare learners for the high flight.

How Vidhyamani works

Vidhyamani is basically a digital platform which plays a role of connector between trainee & educator.

"Vidhyamani" is an online platform that provides smart education to children for their bright future.
Our aim is:

  • Providing good and smart education to children
  • Emphasis on practical knowledge
  • Explain things in a creative way
  • Good education at low cost

Learn With Us

We give a great opportunity to those who does not want to travel and also those who want to study further but for some reason they do not have enough time to study. We facilitate you to study according your suitable time & according to your suitable place.

Earn With Us

“Earn with us” offer instructors a chance to make their career with Vidhyamani. Those who wants to share their skills with the learners, can apply at vidhyamani, they have to only share their resume & a short teaching video on our mail mansi@vidhyamani.com
